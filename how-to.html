How to use Hyphælium

Everything you need to know to get the most from your subscription. Straightforward, honest, no technical jargon.

01 — The idea

What Hyphælium does

You use multiple AI platforms — Claude for deep thinking, ChatGPT for drafting, Gemini for research. Each one holds a piece of your thinking. None of them talk to each other.

Hyphælium sits quietly in the corner of each AI platform. When you have a conversation worth keeping, you click the small green icon once. Hyphælium saves a breadcrumb — the platform, the topic, the link back — to your private vault.

Later, you open your vault and see your complete trail of thought. Click any breadcrumb to return directly to that conversation.

That is all it does. One click. One breadcrumb. Always findable.

02 — Saving a breadcrumb

How to save

1

Have a conversation on any supported AI platform

Claude, ChatGPT, Gemini, Copilot or Perplexity — open any of these in Chrome as normal.

2

Look for the green icon in the bottom right corner

A small dark circular icon with a green glow sits in the corner of the page. It shows a small "Save" label beneath it.

3

Click the icon when the conversation matters

One click. The label briefly shows "✓" and a small message confirms the save. That's it.

Keyboard shortcut: Press Ctrl + Shift + H (Windows) or Cmd + Shift + H (Mac) to save without moving your mouse.

You do not need to save every message. Save when a conversation reaches something worth returning to — a useful answer, a line of research, a decision made.

03 — The vault

Viewing your breadcrumbs

1

Open your vault

Open the file hyphaelium-vault.html in Chrome. Bookmark it for quick access.

2

Sign in with your credentials

Enter your Supabase Project URL and anon key — the same ones you entered in the extension. You only need to do this once per browser.

3

Browse your trail

Your breadcrumbs appear grouped by day, with the platform, topic and time shown for each. Use the filter buttons to narrow by platform or topic.

4

Return to any conversation

Click any breadcrumb to open that conversation directly. It opens in a new tab, exactly where you left it.

Your vault is online: Bookmark hyphaelium.com/vault.html for instant access from any device, any browser.

05 — Thread awareness

What the icon tells you

As your conversation develops, the green icon subtly changes:

Border brightens — the conversation is deepening. More exchanges have taken place.

Border turns amber — the AI has signalled a knowledge boundary. It has used a phrase like "as of my last update" or "you may wish to verify this". This is a useful signal that you might benefit from checking a current source or continuing the thread on a research-focused platform like Perplexity.

Hover over the icon to see a tooltip showing the conversation title, depth, and topic as Hyphælium has understood them.

06 — If something goes wrong

Common questions

The green icon is not appearing.
Make sure you are using Chrome. Refresh the page after enabling the extension. Check that the extension is enabled in chrome://extensions.

The save shows amber and says "saved locally".
Your device was offline when you saved. The breadcrumb has been kept safely on your device and will sync to your vault automatically when you reconnect to the internet.

The vault shows "table not found".
Run the SUPABASE_SCHEMA.sql file in your Supabase SQL Editor. Instructions are in the setup guide.

The vault shows a 401 error.
Your credentials need re-entering. Open the Hyphælium popup, disconnect and reconnect with your Supabase URL and anon key.

Important: Hyphælium only saves in Chrome. If you use a different browser, install the Tampermonkey version — the file hyphaelium.user.js in the tampermonkey folder.
