Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

The plain version: Hyphælium saves only the minimum possible — the platform you used, a short label, and the URL of the conversation. Nothing you typed. Nothing the AI replied. Your data is stored in your own private Supabase vault. We cannot read it.

Who we are

Hyphælium is operated as a sole trader based in Burton upon Trent, United Kingdom. If you have any questions about this policy, contact us at privacy@hyphaelium.com

What data we collect

When you use the Hyphælium browser extension and click the Save button, the following data is recorded:

  • Platform — which AI service you were using (e.g. Claude, ChatGPT)
  • Identity tag — a short label derived from the opening words of your query
  • Route path — the URL of the conversation page
  • Timestamp — the date and time of the save
  • Topic — an automatically classified topic category

We do not collect the content of your queries, the AI's responses, any files you upload to AI platforms, or any personal identifiers beyond those listed above.

Where your data is stored

Your breadcrumbs are stored in a Supabase database. Supabase is a trusted, open-source database platform compliant with GDPR. Your data is stored on servers within the European Union.

Your Supabase credentials (project URL and access key) are stored locally in your browser's encrypted sync storage. They are never transmitted to Hyphælium's servers.

How your data is used

Your data is used solely to provide the Hyphælium service — displaying your breadcrumb trail in your vault and enabling you to return to past conversations. Your data is never sold, shared with third parties, used for advertising, or analysed for any purpose other than providing the service to you.

Your rights under GDPR

As a UK and EU resident you have the right to:

  • Access all data we hold about you
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Export your data in a portable format
  • Withdraw consent at any time

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at privacy@hyphaelium.com and we will respond within 30 days.

Data retention

Your data is retained for as long as you maintain an active Hyphælium subscription. When you cancel your subscription, your data is deleted within 30 days upon request.

Cookies

The Hyphælium landing page does not use tracking cookies. The browser extension uses Chrome's built-in encrypted storage — not cookies — to store your credentials.

Third party services

  • Supabase — database storage. Privacy policy at supabase.com/privacy
  • Stripe — payment processing. Privacy policy at stripe.com/privacy
  • Netlify — website hosting. Privacy policy at netlify.com/privacy

None of these services receive your AI conversation data.

Changes to this policy

We will notify subscribers of any material changes to this policy by email. Continued use of Hyphælium after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact
